Mad Men - About

2007-2015, TV-MA, 7 seasons, 92 episodes

Experience the inner workings of 1960s NY professional and personal lives of those who work in advertising on Madison Avenue - self-coined "mad men" in this innovative drama series created by the producer and writter Matthew Weiner, the heart of these stories is Donald Draper, the creative genius of the company, his deep brilliant beliefs the fact of a troubled childhood, one that he would rather forget and not let anyone know about except for a select few, but one that shaped who he is as an adult and as an ad man in the need not only to sell products but sell himself to the outside world.
